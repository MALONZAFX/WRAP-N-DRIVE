What We Do

Our Services

01
Full Body Wraps

Complete vehicle transformation using premium 3M and KPMF vinyl. Matte, gloss, satin, chrome and custom finishes — applied with precision to every panel.

02
Colour Changes

Completely reinvent your vehicle's look without permanent paint. Reversible colour-change wraps in hundreds of shades — factory-fresh aesthetics, zero commitment.

03
Paint Protection Film

Self-healing PPF shields your paintwork from stone chips, scratches and UV damage. Invisible protection that preserves your car's value for years.

04
Partial Wraps

Roof, bonnet, mirrors, pillars, or any section you choose. Partial wraps deliver high visual impact at a fraction of the full-body cost.

05
Fleet & Corporate Branding

Turn your company vehicles into moving billboards. We handle design, printing and installation for fleets of any size — consistent branding across every vehicle.

06
Mobile Wrapping

Can't come to us? We come to you. Our mobile team provides professional installation across Nairobi and major towns throughout Kenya.

Who We Are

Wrap N Drive

Founded in 2018, Wrap N Drive is Nairobi's most trusted premium vehicle wrapping studio. Based along Mombasa Road in Industrial Area, we've completed over 850 projects — from individual enthusiasts to major corporate fleets.

We work exclusively with 3M, KPMF and Avery Dennison premium vinyl — the same materials used by top automotive studios worldwide. Every wrap is installed by certified technicians in our climate-controlled facility.
